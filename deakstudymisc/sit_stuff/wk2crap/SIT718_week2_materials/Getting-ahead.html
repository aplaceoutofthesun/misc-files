Getting ahead

Putting in a little bit of work up front will help you get ahead with your study.

Over the next couple of weeks we will be extending upon the theories you have learned in these past two weeks and contextualising them to real world scenarios.

Revision of weeks 1 and 2 will help you build up to your first assessment task. In this assessment you will need to analyse a company on the stock exchange and predict the future value of its shares. Completing this task will require you to use R programming, so please be sure that you are comfortable with it.

Activity

Practice programming in R and RStudio and extend your capabilities by doing searching for a relevant short training video in R.

In the discussion forum, discuss interesting clips you found that helped you understand R programming language and also share what you’re looking forward to learning more about in the next couple of weeks.
