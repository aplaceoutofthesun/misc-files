Assessment task 1 : Five bi-weekly quizzes

The quizzes have been designed to align to specific areas of the unit content. Ensure that you have read broadly and have a good knowledge of all learning materials and basic programming functions to set yourself up for success.

In the 'Quiz Guide' (go to Resources>Assessments>Assessment 1) you will find information on how to access and take the quiz, and how to view your submission.  Here you will also find detailed information on Assessment 1 including the open dates for each quiz.  And remember to regularly check the home page for announcements.

Once the quiz is started there will be a limited time for submission, e.g. within 1 hour (details to be provided during the trimester).
