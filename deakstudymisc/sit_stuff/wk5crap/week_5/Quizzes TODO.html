Quizzes

The second of five quizzes that make up assessment task 1, opens this week.

The second quiz will briefly cover the topics addressed in weeks 3 and 4: Distributions, Data Cleaning/Tidy Data, and Transformations. Ensure you attempt the Practice Quiz first as it’ll give you an idea of what to expect for the actual quiz.

Assessment task 1: Five bi-weekly quizzes

Quiz details can be found in the Content tab (go to Content>Assessment Resources>Assessment 1).

Place: Quizzes can be accessed directly via the Assessment menu (go to Assessment>Quizzes).
Duration: Quizzes will remain accessible for 14 days after the open date.

Activity

How have you prepared for the online quizzes? Share your thoughts and ideas with your peers and include the following points in the comments:

  • Do you use any mnemonic techniques to help you remember specific content or ideas?
  • Is there a time of day/night that you find is optimal for thinking?
  • How do you wind down once a quiz has been attempted?

Share and thoughts or seek clarification about the quiz in the assessment discussion forum.
