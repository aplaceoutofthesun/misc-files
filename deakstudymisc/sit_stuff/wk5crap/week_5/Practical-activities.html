Practical activities

The practical activities are aimed towards building confidence with specific programming sequences in R.

In weeks 5 and 6 you will be working on more complex programming functions through the practical exercises. These activities will feed into the completion of the problem solving assessment items.

Weeks 5 and 6 practical activities

The Practical activities (go to Content>Workshops/Practicals) provide opportunity to apply what has been covered in the learning materials. Both Practical 3 and Practical 4 cover this week’s content. Practical 5 covers next week’s content.
