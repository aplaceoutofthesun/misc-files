Quizzes

The first of five assessment quizzes opens this week. Check the Home page for announcements.

The video presents an outline of requirements to successfully complete the online quizzes. Take some time to familiarise yourself with the expectations associated with completing these tasks.

It is important for you to note, that every quiz will have ten multiple choice questions and some questions may have more than one correct answer. Please ensure that all tasks and tutorials have been attempted prior to taking the quiz.

Key points

The following points will help in your preparation for the first quiz:

  • Review relevant week's content.
  • Read broadly.
  • Ensure your internet connection is reliable.
  • Use the practice tests in the Cloud Deakin assessment area.
  • Take some time to mentally prepare so that you are calm when you attempt the quiz.

Activity

Completing tests under time pressure can be stressful at the best of times, particularly if you have not done so for a long time.

Everyone responds differently to these situations and everyone has their own unique strategies for overcoming them. Share your thoughts and ideas with your peers and include the following points in your comment:

  • How long it has been since you have taken a timed test, exam or quiz.
  • How do you feel about them in general?
  • What is your winning strategy for preparing to complete them?

Share and thoughts or seek clarification about the quiz in the discussion forum.
