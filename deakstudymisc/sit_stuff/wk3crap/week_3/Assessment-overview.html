MAIA ANGELOVA TURKEDJIEVA: Welcome to assessments for SIT 718. The assessment tasks for real-world analytics are composed of five quizzes and three assignments. Each component tests different skills and abilities that you will be introduced to in the learning materials, activities, and discussion forums. The assessments have also been designed to test your transferable skills-- in particular, programming, presentation, writing, and summarising information. These professional skills are an essential part of your training as data analysts, as you will need to present your findings and convince your clients of the conclusions you make and the meaning of your results. Best of luck.

Activity
