Practical activities

The practical activities covered in this unit relate to specific content.

Each week you will be presented with learning material that address key theoretical ideas and programming functions. The practicals will help you to unpack them in greater detail.

The practicals involve exercises designed to assist you in applying your understanding of essential content. They are a collation of R tutorials and R-related exercises distributed as tasks throughout the articles.

These activities are designed to be tackled independently and after the theoretical content is covered. However, practicals for weeks 3–6 have been released this week, so that you can work through them at your own pace.

Access the practical activities for weeks 3 and 4

Practical activities 2, 3 and 4 are now available in the program. See the details for each in the unit resource  page (go to Resources>Workshops/Practicals).

  • Practical 2 is focused on content presented this week.
