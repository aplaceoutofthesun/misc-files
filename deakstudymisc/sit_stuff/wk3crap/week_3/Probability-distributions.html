Probability distributions

This topic will give you an overview of some of the more common distributions that occur in data analysis.

To begin this weeks topic probability distributions with the introduction of the use of the histogram as a way to identify some important distributions and learn their characteristics.

This weeks content will help you toward completing the first of the two problem solving assignments. So it is important that you are able to comprehend these concepts and apply them through programming tasks.

Data tends to fall into common patterns or scenarios which can be modelled by various probability distributions. We briefly cover two examples (although there are lots more) for the purposes of recognising what data may look like when modelled as a histogram.

Activity

Several probability distributions exist out there. Research one (that is not the uniform or the normal distribution) and describe a scenario in which it can be applied to.
