Practical activity

The practical activities have been designed to develop skills associated with data transformation. The practical tasks you have been working through encompasses working with whole matrices of data and learning how to implement transformations and power means.

Through practical activities, you will apply concepts associated with data distributions and transformations. Once complete you will be eager to ask a few questions that will help you extend your learning.
