Getting ahead

Good, early preparation is the key to study success, so if you’ve got some time, why not get ready and start planning for Course 3 now?

In weeks 1 and 2 we covered basic aggregation functions, and in weeks 3 and 4 we covered transformations. In weeks 5 and 6 we’ll cover some slightly more advanced models/aggregation functions and ways of measuring how well they fit a given dataset, and will be the most content-heavy course in this program.

Activity

Weeks 5 and 6  will cover content from Chapters 3-5 from the prescribed textbook An Introduction to Data Analysis using Aggregation Functions in R (James 2016) so feel free to read ahead!
